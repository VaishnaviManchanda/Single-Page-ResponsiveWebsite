TRAINER

PROFILE

Global training group (GTG) is the brainchild of Mr. Prashant Shriram Sawant, incepted on 2nd April 2012 as a Corporate Training Organization with a unique focus of actual field learning.

GTG is in the business of providing solutions to Entrepreneur, Employees & Professionals across all evolution phases of their business growth cycle. This is accomplished via providing Education, Training, Coaching, Mentoring, Motivational speeches and related merchandise.

GTG has glorious history & proven credentials of many years in training and inspiring over 20000 participants across a vast spectrum of industries ( Financial services like Banking , Insurance , Telecom, Diamond polishing , Service etc to name few ) and create success stories .

Methodology used includes a mix of classroom training, interactive & field activity based workshops along with individual reviews. The unique differentiator GTG has is its insight into a participant’s mind & focus on driving implementation so that knowledge does not remain as knowledge but finds a business application in the participant’s context, with visible results.

GTG has its office in Mumbai & conducts trainings in various parts of India.

GTG is endowed with intensely talented & passionate Trainers, Coaches who bring with them rich experience of entrepreneurial mindset. Internally GTG constantly nurtures manufactures, certifies and upgrades all its employees, including Trainers for creating unique experience & visible business results for its valued customers.
